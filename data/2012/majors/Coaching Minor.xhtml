Coaching Minor

COLLEGE: Education & Human Sciences

PROGRAM: Coaching (Minor only)

DEGREE: Minor only

HOURS REQUIRED: 15

MINOR AVAILABLE: Yes

DESCRIPTION

This minor is not available for students seeking teacher certification; a supplemental endorsement in coaching is available for students in teacher education.
