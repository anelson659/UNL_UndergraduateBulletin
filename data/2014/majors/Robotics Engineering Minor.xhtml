Robotics Engineering Minor

COLLEGE: Engineering

MAJOR: Robotics Engineering Minor

DEGREE OFFERED: Minor only

HOURS REQUIRED: 16-20

MINIMUM CUMULATIVE GPA:

MINOR AVAILABLE: Yes

CHIEF ADVISER:

DESCRIPTION

Chair and Chief Adviser: Benjamin Terry (MME)

Faculty:  Alisa Gilmore (CEEN), Sebastian Elbaum (CSE), Carrick Detweiler (CSE), Wei Qiao (EE), Carl Nelson (MME), and Shane Farritor (MME)

The Robotics Engineering minor is jointly administered by the Departments of:

  • Computer and Electronics Engineering (CEEN)
  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EE)
  • Mechanical and Materials Engineering (MME)

REQUIREMENTS FOR MINOR OFFERED BY DEPARTMENT

  • This minor is only available to College of Engineering students.

The Robotics Engineering Minor consists of 3 core courses and 3 elective courses.  When selecting electives,the student must take 2 courses outside of their major area of study.  For example, a student in mechanical engineering might take an elective from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and one from the Electrical Engineering Department.

Core Requirements: One course from each of the three following topic areas

Topic Area: Core Programming

  • CSCE 155
  • CSCE 156
  • CIST 1400

Topic Area: Controls

  • MECH 496
  • MECH 350
  • ELEC 444
  • ELEC 222
  • CEEN 491

Topic Area: Embedded Systems

  • CEEN 106
  • CSCE 236
  • MECH 457

Elective Requirements: Three courses from the following list of electives are required; two must be outside your department.

  • ELEC 400
  • ELEC 428
